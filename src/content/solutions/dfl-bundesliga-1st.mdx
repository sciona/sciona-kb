# DFL - Bundesliga Data Shootout - 1st Place (2022) - Pipeline Template

## Problem

Video Action Detection solution template for DFL - Bundesliga Data Shootout - 1st Place (2022), modeled as a 5-stage deterministic pipeline: Frame extraction, 2.5D CNN extraction, SlowFast extraction, Sequence modeling, and downstream competition-specific post-processing.

## Approach

This template captures the ordered steps used by DFL - Bundesliga Data Shootout - 1st Place (2022): prepare the data, build task-specific representations, train or apply the main model, and convert outputs into the final competition submission.

## CDG Walkthrough

{/* <ScionaViewerPlaceholder cdgId="solution.kaggle.dfl_bundesliga_1st" readOnly={true} /> */}

1. **Frame extraction** -- This stage sample videos at 25 FPS and resize to 480p (OpenCV).
1. **2.5D CNN extraction** -- This stage stack 3 consecutive video frames into the RGB channels of a single image and pass through an EfficientNet to extract spatial features (PyTorch).
1. **SlowFast extraction** -- This stage run a SlowFast 3D-CNN directly on a window of video frames to extract temporal features (PyTorchVideo).
1. **Sequence modeling** -- This stage concatenate spatial and temporal features per frame, and pass the sequence through a 1D CNN / Transformer layer (PyTorch).
1. **Non-Maximum Suppression (NMS)** -- This stage locate peaks in the 1D probability output to pinpoint the exact frame of the action (pass, foul, shot) (scipy).

## Key Insight

The load-bearing idea is the pipeline topology: Frame extraction creates the representation needed by Non-Maximum Suppression (NMS), so later stages can optimize the competition metric without solving raw-data ingestion from scratch.

## Failure Modes

- Distribution shift breaks handcrafted thresholds, post-processing rules, or validation-derived weights.
- Missing metadata prevents faithful construction of candidates, groups, windows, or cross-validation folds.
- A weak first-stage recall step cannot be recovered by downstream rescoring or ensembling.
