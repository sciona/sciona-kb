# MICCAI TN-SCUI - 1st Place (2020) - Pipeline Template

## Problem

Semantic segmentation solution template for MICCAI TN-SCUI - 1st Place (2020), modeled as a 5-stage deterministic pipeline: Data Loading, Image Normalization, Segmentation Training, TTA Inference, and downstream competition-specific post-processing.

## Approach

This template captures the ordered steps used by MICCAI TN-SCUI - 1st Place (2020): prepare the data, build task-specific representations, train or apply the main model, and convert outputs into the final competition submission.

## CDG Walkthrough

{/* <ScionaViewerPlaceholder cdgId="solution.kaggle.miccai_tn_scui_1st" readOnly={true} /> */}

1. **Data Loading** -- This stage ingest single-target medical imagery specifically focused on thyroid nodules (PIL, numpy).
1. **Image Normalization** -- This stage apply standard intensity normalization and min-max scaling to standardize the dynamic range of the ultrasound imagery.
1. **Segmentation Training** -- This stage train an encoder-decoder architecture optimized heavily for single-target, high-contrast boundaries (torch).
1. **TTA Inference** -- This stage apply deterministic flips and rotations during prediction to smooth out spatial variance.
1. **Binarization** -- This stage threshold the continuous probability maps into strict binary output masks.

## Key Insight

The load-bearing idea is the pipeline topology: Data Loading creates the representation needed by Binarization, so later stages can optimize the competition metric without solving raw-data ingestion from scratch.

## Failure Modes

- Distribution shift breaks handcrafted thresholds, post-processing rules, or validation-derived weights.
- Missing metadata prevents faithful construction of candidates, groups, windows, or cross-validation folds.
- A weak first-stage recall step cannot be recovered by downstream rescoring or ensembling.
