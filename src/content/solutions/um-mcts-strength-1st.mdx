## Approach

This template captures the ordered steps used by UM Game Playing Strength of MCTS - 1st Place (2024): prepare the data, build task-specific representations, train or apply the main model, and convert outputs into the final competition submission.

1. **Target Smoothing** -- This stage recompute the competition's target labels 5 times to estimate a significantly cleaner, lower-variance "real" target value.
1. **Quantile Regression Training (Lower)** -- This stage train a regressor explicitly targeted toward the negative (-1) direction utilizing a strict quantile loss function (alpha=0.2) (lightgbm or xgboost).
1. **Quantile Regression Training (Upper)** -- This stage train an independent regressor targeted toward the positive (+1) direction using an inverse quantile loss (alpha=0.8).
