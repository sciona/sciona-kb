## Key Insight

## Pipeline Stages

### DICOM windowing :white_check_mark:

This stage convert CT scan slices to 3-channel PNGs mapping different medical windows (lung, mediastinal, PE) to RGB channels (pydicom).

**Bound atom:** `sciona.atoms.bio.medical_imaging_3d.preprocessing.dicom_window`

### Spatial feature extraction :warning:

This stage pass every CT slice through a 2D CNN (EfficientNet/ResNeXt) to extract embeddings (PyTorch).

**Bound atom:** `sciona.atoms.dl.architectures.efficientnet_backbone`

### Sequence modeling :red_circle:

This stage pass the ordered sequence of embeddings from a single patient through a Bidirectional LSTM (PyTorch).

### Multi-head output :red_circle:

This stage project the LSTM hidden states to 9 distinct classification heads (slice-level PE, study-level severity, etc.).

### Post-processing logic :red_circle:

This stage apply rigid competition logic rules to the probabilities to ensure no contradictory predictions (e.g., cannot have severe PE if no PE exists) (numpy).
