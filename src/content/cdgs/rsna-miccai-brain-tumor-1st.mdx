## Key Insight

## Pipeline Stages

### 3D Volume registration :red_circle:

This stage coregister all 4 MRI modalities (FLAIR, T1w, T1Gd, T2w) into exactly the same physical space using a medical imaging registration tool (ANTs/SimpleITK).

### Brain masking :red_circle:

This stage utilize a pre-trained Brain Extraction Tool (BET) to strictly mask out the skull and eyes from the 3D volume (SimpleITK).

### 3D CNN Backbone :red_circle:

This stage train a 3D ResNet/EfficientNet-3D architecture on the volumes (PyTorch / MONAI).

### Multi-plane slicing (2.5D fallback) :red_circle:

This stage extract the middle slices across the Axial, Coronal, and Sagittal planes, and train separate 2D CNNs on each plane (PyTorch).

### Late Fusion :red_circle:

This stage concatenate the 1D embeddings of the 3D model and the 2.5D models into an MLP to predict the MGMT promoter methylation status (genomic binary target).
