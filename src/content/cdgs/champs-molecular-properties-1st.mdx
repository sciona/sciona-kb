## Pipeline Stages

### Spatial Graphing :red_circle:

This stage convert 3D Cartesian coordinates of atoms into distance matrices representing molecular bonds (pandas/numpy).

### Target scaling :white_check_mark:

This stage mathematically standardize the highly variant scalar coupling constants into a normal distribution for stable gradient descent (sklearn).

**Bound atom:** `sciona.atoms.ml.sklearn.preprocessing.standard_scaler_transform`

### Message Passing Neural Network (MPNN) :red_circle:

This stage train a specialized SchNet architecture (a continuous-filter convolutional neural network for modeling quantum interactions) on the atomic nodes and distances (PyTorch).

### Attention mechanism :red_circle:

This stage apply an attention layer to pool the final node embeddings into pair-wise interaction scores (PyTorch).

### Ensembling :red_circle:

This stage average predictions across dozens of MPNN seeds and LightGBM models trained purely on the extracted graph features.
