## Key Insight

The load-bearing idea is the pipeline topology: Data Loading creates the representation needed by Ensemble & TTA, so later stages can optimize the competition metric without solving raw-data ingestion from scratch.

## Pipeline Stages

### Data Loading :red_circle:

This stage ingest microscopy imagery and corresponding bounding box/mask annotations (pandas, PIL).

### Label Harmonization :red_circle:

This stage intentionally include low-confidence "unsure" labels during the training phase to maximize data volume, establishing that pipeline robustness benefits more from dataset scale than from absolute label purity.

### Bounding Box Generation :red_circle:

This stage convert irregular instance masks into tightly bounded box coordinates to serve as targets for the initial detection stage (scikit-image).

### Detection Stage :red_circle:

This stage train a standard 2D object detector to locate broader cell clusters across the slide (torchvision.models.detection).

### Crop Extraction :red_circle:

This stage extract highly specific regions of interest (ROIs) based entirely on the detector's coordinate predictions (torch.tensor).

### Segmentation Stage :red_circle:

This stage train a semantic segmentation model (U-Net architecture) exclusively on the localized crops to output pixel-precise biological masks (segmentation_models_pytorch).

### Ensemble & TTA :red_circle:

This stage average the mask probabilities across multiple spatial scales and horizontal/vertical flips (Test-Time Augmentation).
