## Pipeline Stages

### Belief State Initialization :red_circle:

This stage initialize 100 distinct Beta distributions representing the unknown payout probabilities of the 100 bandit machines (scipy.stats).

### Opponent modeling :red_circle:

This stage algorithmically track the decay of machines specifically triggered by the opponent agent, updating the internal probability map without spending own turns (Python).

### Bayesian UCB sampling :red_circle:

This stage calculate the Upper Confidence Bound (UCB) mathematically to balance exploitation (pulling known good machines) and exploration (testing unknown machines).

### Action execution :white_check_mark:

This stage submit the highest-scoring machine index.

**Bound atom:** `sciona.atoms.inference.bayesian_bandits.select_best_arm`

### Belief updating :red_circle:

This stage receive the reward (0 or 1) from the environment and update the Alpha/Beta parameters of the specific machine's distribution (Python).
