## Key Insight

## Pipeline Stages

### Base Forecasting :red_circle:

This stage generate point predictions for all items using the standard M5 Accuracy methodology (LightGBM with Tweedie loss) (LightGBM).

### Residual calculation :red_circle:

This stage calculate the historical errors (residuals) of the trained model over the validation period (pandas/numpy).

### Probability distribution fitting :red_circle:

This stage fit continuous mathematical distributions (e.g., Gaussian, Negative Binomial) over the grouped residuals to estimate the variance for each product category (scipy.stats).

### Quantile Generation :red_circle:

This stage sample the fitted distributions to calculate the exact 9 discrete target quantiles (e.g., 0.005, 0.50, 0.995) required by the competition (numpy).

### Hierarchical scaling :red_circle:

This stage scale the lower-level quantiles algorithmically so that the statistical spread of the lowest item perfectly aligns with the required variance of the top-level national aggregate (custom math / scipy).
