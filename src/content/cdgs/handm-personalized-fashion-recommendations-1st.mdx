## Pipeline Stages

### Trend generation :white_check_mark:

This stage compute the top 100 most popular items in the trailing 2 weeks (pandas).

**Bound atom:** `sciona.atoms.dl.recommender.item_popularity_decay`

### Collaborative filtering :white_check_mark:

This stage use Alternating Least Squares (ALS) to generate matrix factorization candidates for users (implicit).

**Bound atom:** `sciona.atoms.dl.recommender.als_user_update`

### Candidate merging :red_circle:

This stage union trend candidates, ALS candidates, and historically purchased items into a master pool.

### Feature engineering :red_circle:

This stage calculate user spending power, item price trends, and seasonal overlap (pandas).

### Ranking model :red_circle:

This stage train LightGBM in Ranker mode (Lambdarank) optimizing for MAP@12.
