## Pipeline Stages

### Data alignment :red_circle:

This stage join the 14 different crypto assets along the exact timestamp index, imputing missing minutes with forward-fill (pandas).

### Cross-asset features :red_circle:

This stage compute the ratio of every asset's volume/price relative to Bitcoin to capture broad market momentum (pandas).

### Target neutralization :red_circle:

This stage orthogonalize the training target against the general market movement so the model learns relative performance, not market direction (scipy/numpy).

### Group K-Fold training :red_circle:

This stage split data by time chunks and train LightGBM models independently per asset, plus one unified model across all assets.

### Post-processing :red_circle:

This stage apply the reverse mathematical transformation to un-neutralize the predictions before final submission.
