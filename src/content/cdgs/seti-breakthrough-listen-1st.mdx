## Key Insight

The load-bearing idea is the pipeline topology: Array formatting creates the representation needed by Multi-resolution ensembling, so later stages can optimize the competition metric without solving raw-data ingestion from scratch.

## Pipeline Stages

### Array formatting :red_circle:

This stage reshape the raw signal arrays into 6-channel 2D spatial matrices representing different ON/OFF telescope positions (numpy).

### Image augmentation :red_circle:

This stage apply temporal masking, spatial masking (like SpecAugment), and horizontal flips (Albumentations).

### MixUp :red_circle:

This stage blend random pairs of 6-channel arrays during training to force the model to handle heavily overlapping signals (PyTorch).

### Deep modeling :red_circle:

This stage train EfficientNet-B1, B3, and NFNet architectures on the matrices using BCE loss.

### Multi-resolution ensembling :red_circle:

This stage average predictions of models trained at different image resolutions (e.g., 256x256, 512x512) to catch different signal frequencies.
