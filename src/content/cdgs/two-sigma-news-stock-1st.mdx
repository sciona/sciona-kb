## Key Insight

The load-bearing idea is the pipeline topology: News aggregation creates the representation needed by Tree Modeling, so later stages optimize the metric without solving raw-data routing from scratch.

## Pipeline Stages

### News aggregation :red_circle:

This stage group daily news articles by financial asset, calculating mean sentiment, article volume, and novelty metrics (pandas).

### Market feature engineering :red_circle:

This stage calculate rolling returns, MACD, and Bollinger Bands on the tabular market data (pandas/TA-Lib).

### Future leak prevention :red_circle:

This stage strictly align market data and news data ensuring news arriving after 10 PM is assigned to the next trading day to prevent look-ahead bias.

### Feature concatenation :red_circle:

This stage merge the aggregated NLP features and tabular technical indicators.

### Tree Modeling :red_circle:

This stage train LightGBM models. To prevent models from learning specific eras, train 10 separate models sequentially through time (Walk-Forward Validation) and average the result.
