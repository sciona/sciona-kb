## Pipeline Stages

### 3D Crop/Registration :red_circle:

This stage train a 3D U-Net to locate the exact bounding box of the 7 cervical vertebrae, cropping the original CT scan to isolate the spine (MONAI).

### 2.5D Slice Extraction :red_circle:

This stage extract 3 consecutive slices (Z-axis) into a 3-channel RGB format and pass them through a 2D EfficientNet to extract 1D spatial embeddings (PyTorch).

### Sequence Modeling (Bi-LSTM) :red_circle:

This stage feed the ordered Z-axis embeddings into a Bidirectional LSTM to allow the network to contextually link adjacent bone slices (PyTorch).

### Multi-task heads :red_circle:

This stage attach 8 separate classification heads to the LSTM outputs (one predicting overall patient fracture, seven predicting fractures for specific C1-C7 vertebrae).

### Loss Weighting :red_circle:

This stage apply the competition's exact custom weighting scheme natively in the Cross-Entropy loss function during training to optimize the metric directly.
