## Key Insight

The load-bearing idea is the pipeline topology: Feature Engineering creates the representation needed by Probabilistic Adjustment, so later stages can optimize the competition metric without solving raw-data ingestion from scratch.

## Pipeline Stages

### Feature Engineering :red_circle:

This stage extract time-of-day, weather severity metrics, and historical delay averages from the public feature set (pandas).

### Primary Regression :red_circle:

This stage train a baseline XGBoost regressor to predict the continuous pushback time for each airport (xgboost).

### Residual Calculation :red_circle:

This stage calculate the residuals (errors) between the predicted pushback times and the actual times in the training data.

### Secondary Classification :red_circle:

This stage train a secondary XGBoost classifier specifically designed to detect whether the primary regressor has over-estimated or under-estimated the target based on the residuals.

### Probabilistic Adjustment :red_circle:

This stage use the output probabilities of the secondary classifier to mathematically adjust the continuous predictions of the primary regressor (numpy).
