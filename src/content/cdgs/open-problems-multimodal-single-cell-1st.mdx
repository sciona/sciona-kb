## Pipeline Stages

### Sparse loading :red_circle:

This stage ingest ultra-high-dimensional gene expression and chromatin accessibility datasets utilizing highly compressed sparse row (CSR) matrices (scipy.sparse).

### Dimensionality reduction :white_check_mark:

This stage fit Truncated SVD models independently to both biological modalities to compress 100,000+ columns down to 512 dense components (sklearn).

**Bound atom:** `sciona.atoms.ml.sklearn.decomposition.truncated_svd_fit`

### Cross-modal projection :red_circle:

This stage train a multi-layer perceptron to map the SVD components of Modality 1 into the SVD space of Modality 2 (PyTorch).

### K-Nearest Neighbors smoothing :white_check_mark:

This stage run a KNN regressor over the predicted space to smooth out individual cell predictions based on their spatial proximity to known cell clusters (cuml).

**Bound atom:** `sciona.atoms.ml.sklearn.neighbors.nearest_neighbors_fit`

### Matrix reconstruction :red_circle:

This stage inverse-transform the predicted components back into the original 100,000+ dimensional sparse space (scipy/sklearn).
