This stage train Vision Transformers (ViT) and ResNeXt models (PyTorch/HuggingFace).

### Robust Loss :red_circle:

This stage utilize Bi-Tempered Logistic Loss and Label Smoothing to prevent the model from confidently fitting the mislabeled dataset images.
