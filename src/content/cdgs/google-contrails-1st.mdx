## Pipeline Stages

### Temporal Windowing :red_circle:

This stage extract a window of 5 consecutive satellite frames surrounding the target evaluation time (numpy).

### False-Color generation :red_circle:

This stage apply atmospheric physics formulas (Ash morphology) to combine infrared bands into false-color RGB representations (numpy).

### 2.5D Stacking :red_circle:

This stage instead of a heavy 3D CNN, stack the temporal frames into the channel dimension of a 2D CNN (e.g., 5 frames x 3 channels = 15 channel input) (PyTorch).

### U-Net Architecture :red_circle:

This stage train a U-Net with a MaxViT or ConvNeXt backbone on the 15-channel image to predict the single mask for the center frame (segmentation_models_pytorch).

### Temporal TTA :red_circle:

This stage apply Test Time Augmentation by shifting the temporal window forward and backward, then averaging the output masks.
