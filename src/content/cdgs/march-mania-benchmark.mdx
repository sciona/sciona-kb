## Key Insight

The load-bearing idea is the pipeline topology: Season aggregation creates the representation needed by Logistic Regression, so later stages optimize the metric without solving raw-data routing from scratch.

## Pipeline Stages

### Season aggregation :red_circle:

This stage aggregate regular-season box scores into team-level offensive and defensive efficiency metrics (points per possession, turnover rate) (pandas).

### Graph centralities :red_circle:

This stage construct a directed graph where nodes are teams and edges are games won/lost. Calculate PageRank to determine true strength-of-schedule (NetworkX).

### Elo Ratings :red_circle:

This stage simulate the regular season chronologically to generate continuous Elo ratings for all teams entering the tournament (custom Python).

### Pairwise concatenation :red_circle:

This stage for every possible tournament matchup, concatenate Team A features with Team B features, alongside the difference between their features (pandas).

### Logistic Regression :red_circle:

This stage train a calibrated Logistic Regression model (or XGBoost with log-loss objective) to strictly output the probability that Team A beats Team B.
