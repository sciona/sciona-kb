# Spread_leap_second_into_fractional_day

> Detects a leap-second jump by comparing today's and tomorrow's TAI-UTC offsets and distributes any difference uniformly across the preceding day by adjusting the fractional day fd. This smears the leap second into the 86400 seconds of the current day.
