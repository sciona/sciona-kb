# Calculate_vector_angle

> Computes the angle between two vectors.

## Conceptual Summary

angular_separation. Quantifies the rotational difference between two ordered, n-dimensional numerical sequences. The operation projects two vectors onto a shared 2D plane and measures the angle between them, effectively reducing their relational information to a single scalar representing their relative orientation.. Applications: In natural language processing, measuring the semantic similarity between two documents represented as high-dimensional word embeddings, where a smaller angle implies a closer conceptual relationship., In bioinformatics, calculating the dihedral angles between planes formed by bonded atoms in a protein backbone to determine its secondary structure., In computer vision and robotics, determining the orientation difference between a camera's view vector and a target object's normal vector to guide navigation or manipulation., In financial analysis, assessing the correlation drift between two time-series of asset returns by treating them as vectors in a high-dimensional space.

```
(u: numpy.ndarray, v: numpy.ndarray) -> float
```
