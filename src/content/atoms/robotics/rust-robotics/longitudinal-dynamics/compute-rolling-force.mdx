# Compute_rolling_force

> Compute rolling resistance force from grade angle.

## Conceptual Summary

orientation_modulated_resistive_scalar. Transforms a single orientation angle into a resistive scalar by applying a trigonometric projection from an inclined frame to its normal component, then scaling to model force units. The mapping is a closed-form arithmetic step that reduces effective resistance as the surface/frame normal component decreases with increasing inclination magnitude.. Applications: Mobile robotics: estimating contact-loss-adjusted drag terms for motion planning on tilted substrates., Conveyor/process engineering: computing orientation-dependent resistance terms for material transport on inclined paths., Biomechanics/prosthetics: modeling posture-angle-dependent dissipative load components during gait or assistive actuation., Aerospace/attitude systems: projecting orientation-dependent parasitic load terms in simplified vehicle dynamics.
