# Compute_gravity_grade_force

> Compute gravity-induced force component along the road grade.

## Conceptual Summary

signed_trigonometric_projection_of_constant_vector_field. Transforms an orientation scalar into the directed component of an otherwise constant-magnitude vector field along a chosen axis. The operation applies a trigonometric projection (typically proportional to sin(theta)) with a fixed scale factor from model constants, yielding a signed scalar that indicates direction and intensity along that axis. This is a coordinate-resolution step from angular representation to 1D effective loading.. Applications: Resolving a uniform field load onto a tilted microfluidic channel axis to estimate net transport-driving component., Computing the component of a constant inertial/body-force vector along a robotic link’s motion axis from joint orientation., Projecting environmental acceleration onto an instrument-sensitive axis in inertial sensing and calibration pipelines., Estimating downslope tangential stress component from terrain inclination in geomechanical stability screening.
