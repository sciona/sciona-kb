## Conceptual Summary

ImmutableStatePolicyParameterUpdate. Constructs a new state object by functionally copying an immutable configuration container and replacing a targeted subset of policy parameters. The transform performs deterministic field substitution for a cadence scalar, an event-detection enable flag, a non-negative detection threshold, and a non-negative suppression interval. No historical accumulation or iterative optimization occurs; output is a single-step reparameterized state..
