# Heart_rate_computation

> Compute instantaneous heart rate in bpm from R-R intervals with optional smoothing.

## Conceptual Summary

SequenceToInstantaneousRate. Given a monotonically increasing 1D sequence of event indices, this operation computes the instantaneous frequency for each interval between consecutive events. It first calculates the deltas (differences) between adjacent indices, then computes the reciprocal of each delta to yield a rate. The resulting rate values are indexed to the midpoint of the interval from which they were derived.. Applications: Neuroscience: Calculating the instantaneous firing rate of a neuron from a series of recorded spike timestamps., Mechanical Engineering: Determining the instantaneous rotational speed (RPM) of a shaft from the sequence of pulses generated by a rotary encoder., Geophysics: Estimating the frequency of aftershocks or other seismic events based on their discrete arrival times., Finance: Analyzing the instantaneous rate of high-frequency trades for a security from their transaction timestamps.
