# Bandpass_filter

> Apply FIR bandpass filter (3-45 Hz) to remove baseline wander and high-frequency noise from the raw ECG signal.

## Conceptual Summary

Frequency Band Isolation Transform. A transformation on a 1D ordered numerical vector that attenuates signal components whose frequencies lie outside a specified range. It functions as a selective sieve, preserving cyclical patterns within a target periodicity band while suppressing both low-frequency drifts and high-frequency noise.. Applications: Isolating specific seismic wave frequencies from raw seismograph readings to identify earthquake epicenters., Extracting the frequency range of a specific instrument or voice from a complex audio mix for audio engineering., Detrending financial time-series data by removing long-term market cycles and short-term noise to analyze mid-term business cycle volatility., Filtering radio telescope data to isolate the spectral emission lines of specific chemical elements, removing background cosmic noise and instrumental drift.
