# Averageqrstemplate

> Computes averaged QRS complex template by aligning and averaging heartbeat waveforms around R-peaks

## Conceptual Summary

Landmark-Aligned Ensemble Waveform Averaging. Extracts a prototypical pattern from a repetitive signal by windowing segments around predefined landmarks, aligning them temporally to a common reference frame, and computing the pointwise mean across all instances. This reduces noise and variability to isolate the stable, recurring structure while discarding transient or uncorrelated components.. Applications: Seismology: Generate station-specific earthquake waveform templates by averaging seismic traces aligned to P-wave or S-wave arrival times for matched filter detection, Neuroscience: Construct spike waveform templates for extracellular neural recordings by aligning and averaging action potentials at their peaks for multi-unit spike sorting, Astronomy: Create standard supernova or variable star light curve templates by time-aligning and averaging multiple observations at peak luminosity or phase markers, Industrial vibration analysis: Build reference vibration signatures for rotating machinery by averaging acceleration waveforms aligned to shaft rotation markers for anomaly detection
