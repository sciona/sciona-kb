# Assemblezz2018sqi

> Builds the final ZZ2018 signal-quality result by combining beat, frequency, and kurtosis evidence.

## Conceptual Summary

Band-Limited Spectral Energy Ratio Scorer. Transforms a 1-D sampled scalar sequence into the frequency domain using segment-based spectral estimation, then aggregates power within two user-defined frequency intervals. It maps these aggregated energies to a single scalar via a selectable ratio/normalization rule, quantifying how strongly energy is concentrated in one band relative to another. The operation is a lossy projection from a high-dimensional spectrum to a compact quality index.. Applications: Condition monitoring: quantify fault-signature harmonic concentration versus broadband background in rotating machinery vibration signals., Geophysics: score microseismic event quality by contrasting event-band energy against noise-band energy in single-station traces., Wireless systems: estimate channel occupancy quality by comparing target sub-band power to adjacent/interference bands in RF captures., Acoustics: evaluate tonal prominence versus ambient noise in environmental or industrial sound measurements.
