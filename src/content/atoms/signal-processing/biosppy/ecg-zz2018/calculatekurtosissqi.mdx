# Calculatekurtosissqi

> Calculates a Signal Quality Index (kSQI) based on the statistical kurtosis of the signal.

## Conceptual Summary

MeasureDistributionTailedness. Computes the fourth standardized moment of a 1D numerical array. This scalar value quantifies the 'tailedness' of the distribution, measuring the propensity for values to be concentrated far from the mean (outliers) relative to a Gaussian distribution. A boolean flag selects between the raw moment and the excess moment (adjusted by a constant).. Applications: Quantitative Finance: Assessing the tail risk of an asset's return distribution. High kurtosis indicates a greater probability of extreme market movements than predicted by simpler models., Industrial Process Control: Monitoring sensor data for anomalies. A change in the kurtosis of pressure or temperature readings can signal a deviation from stable operation and predict system failures., Geophysics: Characterizing the impulsiveness of seismic signals. The kurtosis of ground acceleration time-series helps differentiate between background noise and sharp, high-impact tremor events., Neuroscience: Analyzing neuronal firing patterns. The kurtosis of inter-spike interval distributions can distinguish between regular, rhythmic firing and bursty, irregular activity.
