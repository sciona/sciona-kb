# Iterate_pdb_atoms

> Iterates over all atoms in the PDB structure, optionally filtering by a specific chemical element.

## Conceptual Summary

filtered_collection_iterator. Sequentially traverses a structured collection of records, yielding a stateful iterator over the entire set. An optional filtering parameter allows for the selective retrieval of records where a specific categorical attribute matches a given value. This provides a memory-efficient mechanism for processing either the entire dataset or a specific subset without loading the full collection into memory.. Applications: In computer graphics, iterating through all nodes in a scene graph, with an option to filter for nodes of a certain type, like 'light', 'mesh', or 'camera'., In social network analysis, streaming through a dataset of user profiles, selectively yielding only users who match a specific demographic attribute (e.g., city or listed interest)., In financial data processing, scanning a large ledger of transactions and yielding only those associated with a particular transaction type or currency., In astronomical surveys, iterating through a catalog of celestial objects and filtering to return only objects of a specific class, such as 'galaxy' or 'quasar'.
