# Solutionextraction

> Processes the raw results from the quantum sampler. It interprets the measurement count distribution to extract the top solutions for the optimization problem.

## Conceptual Summary

MostProbableStateDecoder. Filters a discrete probability distribution to identify the 'K' most frequently observed states. It then decodes these high-frequency states from a raw format into a structured representation, effectively reducing a large outcome space to a small set of the most likely results.. Applications: Genomics: Identifying the most common genetic variants in a population from sequencing count data., Natural Language Processing: Extracting the most likely candidate words from a language model's output probability distribution for use in beam search decoding., Failure Analysis: Pinpointing the most frequent error codes from system logs to prioritize investigation of system failure modes., Market Research: Determining the most popular product choices from a large set of customer transaction data.
