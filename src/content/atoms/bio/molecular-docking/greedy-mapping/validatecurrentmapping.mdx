# Validatecurrentmapping

> Evaluate whether the current mapping/unmapping pair satisfies graph-lattice consistency constraints.

## Conceptual Summary

Bidirectional Relation Consistency Validator. Consumes a candidate pair of forward and reverse mappings between two discrete relational structures and tests whether they satisfy a shared constraint system. It verifies inverse coherence (round-trip consistency) and structural admissibility against topology/order constraints encoded in the context object. The result is a single feasibility predicate indicating whether the mapping pair is a valid structure-preserving correspondence.. Applications: Verifying correspondence between segmented regions in multimodal medical images under anatomical adjacency constraints, Checking consistency of symbol-to-state alignments in compiler intermediate-representation lowering with reverse traceability guarantees, Validating entity alignment between heterogeneous knowledge graphs while preserving ontology hierarchy relations, Confirming mesh-to-skeleton attachment maps in computational biomechanics with inverse lookup and topology constraints
